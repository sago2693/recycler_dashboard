## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Display category breakdown
The overview dashboard SHALL display a breakdown of collected items by material category. Category names SHALL be displayed in Spanish. The chart SHALL NOT display text labels adjacent to each slice; only the legend or tooltip SHALL identify each category.

#### Scenario: Chief views category distribution
- **WHEN** the chief views the overview dashboard
- **THEN** a pie or bar chart shows the percentage and quantity of each material category collected, with category names in Spanish and no external text labels on the chart slices
